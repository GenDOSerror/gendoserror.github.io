Unique Creations

Every dish is a savory, elegant creation by our sushi chefs. All from the freshest ingredients.

Since 2022, Rami's Ocean of Sushi has offered a selection of innovative twists to the classics of Japanese cuisine. The delicacies range from a myriad of flavorful appetizers, top-of-the-line sushi and sashimi, and renown "Rockin' Sushi."

Rami's Ocean of Sushi was featured in the Channel 12 News as one of the "Top Sushi Studio in City." Also, Rami's Ocean of Sushi has been seen in various magazines.

For lunch, it's a great spot to grab a quick lunch or to just relax and catch up with friends.

For Dinner, it's a trendy, fun, and loud environment to pre game with your friends before a night out.
